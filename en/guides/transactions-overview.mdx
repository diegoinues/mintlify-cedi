## Transfers Module

You access the **"Transfers"** module from the system side menu. Upon entering, the system displays the main screen, where the key elements necessary for managing your operations are presented.

In this view, the system displays your **CLABE**, the **available balance**, as well as the main action buttons: **"New transfer"**, **"International transfer"**, and **"Download bank statement"**. This information allows you to have immediate control over your resources and available options.

---

You can view your CLABE at any time, as well as check the updated available balance. To start a national transfer, you must select the **"New transfer"** option.

---

### Recent movements

The system presents the **"Recent Movements"** section, where the history of completed transactions is displayed. In this section, you can search and filter the information according to different criteria, such as name, date, type, or status.

Once the filters have been applied, the system displays the list of recent transfers, allowing you to review the details of each operation in a clear and organized manner.
