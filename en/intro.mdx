## What is CEDI?

CEDI is the internal system that hosts official documentation for processes related to the operation, administration, and support of the platform. It aims to standardize procedures, support training, and serve as a technical reference for the teams involved.

## Main content

- **User management** — User creation, 2FA code activation, and platform usage.
- **Documented processes** — Step-by-step flows for registration, transfers, disbursements, invoices, and more.
- **Visual flow** — Diagrams exported from Figma for quick reference.
